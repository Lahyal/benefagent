FAQ

Common questions

Do you need access to my employer's HR system?
No. You simply upload a photo or PDF of your benefits packet — the same document your employer sends during onboarding or open enrollment. We don't require any login credentials or system access.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your benefits documents are processed in memory and not stored after analysis. We never sell or share your data. All connections are encrypted end-to-end.
What if my employer doesn't offer all these benefits?
BenefAgent adapts to whatever your employer offers. If you don't have an HSA, we skip that section. The app focuses on what's actually available to you and finds the maximum value within those options.
How is this different from my employer's HR portal?
HR portals show you what you have. BenefAgent tells you what to do with it — in plain English, with specific dollar amounts and prioritized actions. We work on your behalf, not your employer's.
When should I use BenefAgent?
Open enrollment season is the highest-impact time, but BenefAgent is useful year-round — whenever you get a raise, have a life event (marriage, new child), or just want to check you're not leaving money behind.

Frequently asked questions

What is a benefits optimizer?
A benefits optimizer is a tool that analyzes your employee benefits package and identifies unclaimed money — like uncaptured 401k match, unused HSA/FSA funds, or forgotten perks like gym stipends and tuition reimbursement.
What are the 2026 HSA contribution limits?
For 2026, the HSA contribution limit is $4,300 for individuals and $8,550 for families. If you're 55 or older, you can contribute an additional $1,000 catch-up contribution.
How does a 401k retirement calculator work?
A 401k calculator estimates your monthly payout at retirement based on your current balance, contribution rate, employer match, and expected growth rate. BenefAgent's calculator also shows you how much you're missing if you're not capturing your full employer match.
What is BenefAgent and how does it work?
BenefAgent is an AI-powered benefit agent that reads your employer benefits document and creates a personalized action plan. Upload your benefits PDF, answer 5 questions, and get exact dollar amounts for what you're missing — in under 10 minutes.
